== !=	Equality operators	Left

&	Bitwise AND	Left

^	Bitwise XOR	Left

|	Bitwise OR	Left

&&	Logical AND	Left

||	Logical OR	Left

?:	Ternary conditional	Right

= += -= *= /= %= &= ^= |= <<= >>=	Assignment and compound assignment	Right
